"words can describe a human but actions, they define them". Something to ponder upon.

This year Delhi has been ruthless to the poor and homeless, going on a record breaking streak of more than a century for blazing cold winters.

We fall back upon to our warm quilts and hot cocoa at nights but destitue cannot. So this year we would like to introduce a new initiative by us "*KARN*".

Like the selfless character from the epic Mahabharata, let's be generous and charitable this winter.

please contribute anything you find suitable for this noble cause be it 100,200 or even 500, so that quilts can be donated to the poor.

We will be transparent throughout the process by furnishing a copy of the bill and the contact information of the seller.

Hope our ideology resonates with you. "Help us Help others". Let's make a small change, a definitive change, a change to change the world.

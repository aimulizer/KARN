VISION

"A kind gesture can reach a wound that only compassion can heal"

KARN is an initiative by the students of Bharati Vidyapeeth's college of Engineering to assist the folks in dire need of our help. We as an earthborn have one single purpose in life, to help the unfortunate and make their lives worth living.

Working towards this ideology we have defined our first manifesto this winter. So let us provide a soulder for those in need and bring the planet one step closer to the distant utopian world we all envision. And remember there is no exceercise better for heart than reaching down and lifting people up.
